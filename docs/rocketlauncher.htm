  • Rocket Launcher -


  • Homing Rocket Launcher -
    • The alternate Rocket Launcher. Aim in someone's general direction and fire; it'll do the rest, we really mean it.

  • Skipper Homing Rocket -
    • Another alternate Rocket Launcher. Does anyone remember the original Wing Commander? We did, and we recreated the skipper torpedoes from the game. Basically these bad boys will cloak and uncloak until they hit an enemy. But watch out they have a mind of their own and will chase you if provoked.

  • Guided Missile -
    • Another alternate to the rocket launcher. When you fire this rocket you will chase the rocket and guide it with your mouse. Another camper killer weapon.

  • Spiral Rocket Launcher -
    • This is another alternate to the rocket launcher. It shoots three rockets. There is the main rocket in the center while the other two spiral around the first. It makes for an interesting effect.

  • Guided Nuke -
    • A guided version of the Railgun Nuke. Death from above!
