Angel of Mercy
Command: angelom
Usage: bind angelom to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Mercy will catch projectiles for you, sometimes. This angel protects against the following weapons: floating mines, plasma, grenade, rocket, bfg blast, bolt, super shotgun, buckey ball, flare, spike, positron, pulse, homing rocket, guided rocket, guided nuke, nuke. Angel of Mercy also protects against Angel of Frost.
