- Angel of Life

Command: angelol

Usage: bind angelol to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Life will pick up items for you whenever you are near enough to them.

 

- Angel of Death

Command: angelod

Usage: Bind angelod to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Death will shoot in parallel with your shot in the direction you are aiming.

- Angel of Mercy

Command: angelom

Usage: bind angelom to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Mercy will catch projectiles for you, sometimes. Angel of Mercy also protects against Angel of Frost. 

- Angel of Frost

Command: angelof

Usage: bind angelof to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Frost will freeze a person when they are close enough to you. Angel of Mercy or Angel of UnFreeze protects against Angel of Frost.

- Angel of Flame

Command: angeloflame

Usage: bind angeloflame to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Flame will set a person on fire when they are close enough to you. Angel of Life protects against Angel of Flame.

- Angel of Plague

Command: angelop

Usage: bind angelop to a key or type it into the console. When you want to use it press the bound key. When an opponent comes within range the Angel Of Plague rapidly depletes his health until he has only 10 health points left, making him an easy
frag. Flying the Angel of Life makes you immune to plague.

 

- Angel of Blindness

Command: angeloblind

Usage: bind angeloblind to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Blindness will cause the affected person to go blind for a short while when they are close enough to you.

- Angel of Horror

Command: angelohorror

Usage: bind angelohorror to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Horror will cause the affected person to see colors at random when they are close enough to you.

- Angel of Revenge

Command: angelor

Usage: bind angelor to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Revenge rapidly depletes a player's health when you are close enough to him. The angel will take off health until they have 50 health points left. Very effective against regen if you can get close. Flying the Angel of Revenge makes you immune to revenge.

- Angel of Energy

Command: angeloe

Usage: bind angeloe to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Energy will catch energy bolts and plasma rays when they are close enough to you.

 

- Angel of Unfreeze

Command: angelou

Usage: bind angelou to a key or type it into the console. When you do want to use it press the bound key. The Angel Of Unfreeze will unfreeze you immediately and give you some protection against freezing weapons.  Angel of Unfreeze DOES NOT protect against Angel of Frost.
