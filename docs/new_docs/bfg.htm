  • BFG -
    • Big "Fun" Gun, for you and not the opponent!

    • An alternate to the BFG10K. When it shoots it's lasers it will freeze the people that it hits instead of killing them

    • Another alternate to the BFG. When you use this you can vacuum in anything that is not attached to the ground. When I say everything I mean everything.

    • Launch this and run, very, very fast or hook onto something solid. Anything that gets within its radius will get sucked in and crushed. It's pretty uncomfortable.
