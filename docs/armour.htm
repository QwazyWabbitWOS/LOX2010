Armour

Organic Armor If luck has it you'll find the Organic Armor that regenerates itself until it reaches 500. You have a 1 in 1000 chance in picking up this armor.
Slug Armor Are you tired of being snipped or showered by a storm of slugs? Well maybe you are lucky enough to pick up one of these armors. You have a 1 in 1000 chance to pick up one of these.
Cell Armor Looking for protection against the Positron or Freezer? With this new armor you can enjoy yourself at their expense. You have a 300 to 1 chance of getting this armor. When you get this armor players using the Positron and Freezer will get a nice surprise.
Shell Armor Same as Slug and Cell armor but protects against Shell weapons.
Bullet Armor Same as Slug and Cell armor but protects against Bullet weapons.
Rocket Armor Same as Slug and Cell armor but protects against Rocket weapons.
Grenade Armor Same as Slug and Cell armor but protects against Grenade weapons.
Regeneration Health Your health will increase to 400 and regenerate when depleted. You have a 100 to 1 chance of getting this health pack.
Vampiric Health Just like Dracula your health increases as you inflict damage to your enemies and yourself. You have a 100 to 1 chance of getting this health pack.
